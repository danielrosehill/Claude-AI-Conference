
### Focus Areas
- Adoption is far too slow
- Untapped potential
- What we could be doing
- Vision for accelerated adoption
- "Cavemen who think we're high-tech"

### Prompt Questions
- Why do you think our AI adoption is incredibly slow?
- What are we missing that we could be doing?
- Where should we be that we aren't?
- What would aggressive AI adoption look like?

### Notes
This perspective argues: "Yeah, we're using AI, but nowhere near as much as we could be." Views current adoption as primitive compared to what's possible. Should be provocative and challenge the audience's assumptions about how advanced they are.
